IIHA is a project conducted at the **NICC** to study recidivism and criminal careers from digital criminal justice archives.

I contributed to the proposal and scientific management of the project as **co-PI**. The work relied on data from the **Central Criminal Record** and the prison database.

The project played an important role in developing a more robust data infrastructure for the study of criminal trajectories.
