KG4J is a project submitted in 2025 within the framework of the **P4Science** call from the Belgian Federal Science Policy (**BELSPO**). I am the principal investigator and the author of the proposal.

The project aims to explore the contribution of **knowledge graphs** for integrating justice data, structuring heterogeneous information, and improving analytical capabilities.

The consortium brings together academic and institutional partners in Belgium, Canada, and the United States, as well as the **Digital Transformation Office** of the FPS Justice.
