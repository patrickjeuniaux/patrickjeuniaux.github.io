Hello! Welcome to my personal website. I am a scientist working on information systems.

For instance, I am developing a computer system to facilitate the integration of data originating from different criminal justice databases. In general, I am interested by computers, psychology, language and mathematics.

Here, you'll find information about my academic activities by consulting the publications I took part in, and the institutions that supported me.

My research activities belong to two broad categories:

  1. Cognitive Science (experimental psychology and computational linguistics).
  2. Science & Justice (forensic science and criminology).

At the moment, I am a researcher at the National Institute for Criminalistics and Criminology (NICC), and a teaching assistant at UCLouvain.
