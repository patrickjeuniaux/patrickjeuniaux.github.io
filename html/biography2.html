Louvain-la-Neuve

Belgium

I obtained my Bachelor's degree, and my Master's degree, in psychology and sciences of education, with a specialization in cognitive psychology, from la Faculté de psychologie et des sciences de l'éducation (Department of psychology and educational sciences, PSP), at Université catholique de Louvain (UCL) in Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium. My master thesis was done on human reasoning, under the supervision of Prof. Dr Jean Costermans.

In the same University, I later completed my training by obtaining a complementary degree (M.A.) in statistics at the Institut de Statistique, Biostatistique et Sciences Actuarielles (Institute of Statistics, Biostatistics and Actuarial Sciences, ISBA) at UCL.

On the professional side, I worked as a research and teaching assistant at the Institut d'Administration et de Gestion (IAG – Louvain School of Management, UCL), for Prof. Dr Michel Mouchart and Prof. Dr Marie-Paule Kestemont, developping web-based pedagogical tools in PHP and MySQL to teach statistics. I also worked at the Service d'Aide (Support Service, UCL) as a teaching tutor for students with visual disabilities.


— 10 Feb 2018
