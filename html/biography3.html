Leuven

Belgium

I obtained a complementary degree in Artificial Intelligence (MAI) at de Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (University of Leuven, KU Leuven), in Leuven, Belgium.

At KU Leuven, I worked as a research assistant in the Language Intelligence and Information retrieval (LLIR) research group under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Marie-Francine Moens in het Interdisciplinair Centrum voor Recht en Informatie (Interdisciplinary Centre for Law & ICT, ICRI), in de Faculteit Rechtsgeleerdheid (Faculty of Law). We worked on technologies for information extraction from texts, involving reference resolution.
