I worked as a postdoctoral researcher on support systems and complex cognition in Quebec City, Canada in le laboratoire `Cognition – Distribution – Organisation – Technologies´ (Co-DOT, a cognitive ergonomics research laboratory), under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sébastien Tremblay at l'École de psychologie (School of Psychology) at Université Laval (Laval University).

There I collaborated with partners from le Centre de recherches de Valcartier (Valcartier Research Centre) within la Recherche et développement pour la défense Canada (Defence Research and Development Canada, DRDC-RDDC), and Thales.
